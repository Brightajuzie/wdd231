NP Membership Benefits (No Fee)

This membership is for non-profit organizations and includes basic access to resources:

  • Access to the Monthly Newsletter.
  • Inclusion in the online member directory.
  • 5% discount on Annual Conference tickets.

Bronze Membership Benefits

The Bronze level provides enhanced support for small businesses:

  • All NP Membership benefits.
  • Free access to one online training workshop per year.
  • 10% discount on all special events.
  • Listing in the printed member directory.

Silver Membership Benefits

The Silver level offers significant promotional and training opportunities:

  • All Bronze Membership benefits.
  • Free access to all online training workshops.
  • Opportunity for a "Member Spotlight" position on the home page once per year.
  • Dedicated networking sessions.

Gold Membership Benefits

The Gold level is our premium offering, providing maximum exposure and value:

  • All Silver Membership benefits.
  • 25% discount on all advertising packages.
  • Priority registration for all events.
  • Exclusive invitation to the annual "Gold Circle" reception with the board members.
  • Dedicated account manager.
